# External Reference Links

These are the primary source documents that inform this repository. They are publicly available from government and industry sources. We link to them here rather than storing large PDFs in git.

## Federal regulations

- **24 CFR Part 3285 — Model Manufactured Home Installation Standards**
  https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-24/subtitle-B/chapter-XX/part-3285

- **24 CFR Part 3280 — Manufactured Home Construction and Safety Standards (MHCSS)**
  https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-24/subtitle-B/chapter-XX/part-3280

- **24 CFR Part 3282 — Manufactured Home Procedural and Enforcement Regulations**
  https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-24/subtitle-B/chapter-XX/part-3282
