# Public Lane

This lane contains publishable material derived from the internal workflow. It is meant to be useful for clients, processors, appraisers, developers, contractors, and homeowners without exposing client evidence or internal-only judgment notes.

## Rules for this lane

- Keep it educational and reusable.
- Do not include case-specific data.
- Do not include internal escalation heuristics that would be misleading without engineering context.
- Prefer clear checklists, glossaries, and guide content over internal process jargon.
