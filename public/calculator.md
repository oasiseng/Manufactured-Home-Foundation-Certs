# Calculator Companion

The repo intentionally keeps the existing manufactured-home calculator as a linked companion rather than absorbing it into this codebase on day one.

## Why it stays separate for now

- This repository is centered on operations, evidence handling, and workflow consistency.
- The calculator is already a distinct technical utility with its own improvement path.
- Keeping the projects separate makes it easier to publish educational content without mixing it with internal production workflow.
