# Remote Evidence Checklist

Send the strongest package you can. Remote certification works best when the engineer can clearly see the support system, site conditions, and any attached improvements.

## Please include

- appraisal report or lender property summary
- exterior photos from all sides of the home
- close and mid-range photos of the foundation or support system
- crawlspace or underside photos if the area is accessible
- photos and notes for decks, porches, stairs, carports, garages, ramps, or additions
- survey, plot plan, or simple site sketch if available

## Photo tips

- Take photos during daylight if possible.
- Include at least one photo per side from far enough back to show grade and skirting.
- Take extra underside photos at corners and mid-span support locations.
- If an area is blocked, include a photo showing why it cannot be accessed.
- Do not send blurry screenshots when original photos are available.

## What may trigger a site visit

- no usable underside visibility
- unclear support type
- drainage or settlement concerns
- attached structures with unclear structural independence
- lender or jurisdiction requiring in-person verification
