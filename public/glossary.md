# Glossary

## AHJ

Authority having jurisdiction. The local body that enforces permitting or code requirements.

## Anchorage

The components and connections that resist uplift, sliding, or overturning.

## Boxabl

Used in this repo as a shorthand for Boxabl-specific review paths that depend on the product configuration and manufacturer basis.

## Crawl space

The accessible or partially accessible underfloor area below the home.

## HUD

The U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development. HUD standards and guidance are central to many manufactured-home installation and lending questions.

## PFGMH

Permanent Foundations Guide for Manufactured Housing. Commonly referenced when determining whether an existing installation aligns with permanent-foundation expectations.

## Remote review

A desk-based engineering review using submitted evidence instead of an in-person site visit.

## Site tech

Field personnel sent to document conditions that are too unclear for a conservative remote-only conclusion.
