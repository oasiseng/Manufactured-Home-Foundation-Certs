# Boxabl Accessory Scope Checklist

Boxabl jobs can look simple until the accessory scope starts carrying structural risk. Use this checklist before treating stairs, decks, or other improvements as incidental.

## Confirm each item

- Is the accessory attached to the main module or structurally independent?
- Does the accessory introduce new concentrated loads into the support path?
- Does the accessory require its own footing, landing, or lateral restraint strategy?
- Are guard, landing, and stair requirements part of the requested engineering scope?
- Is the accessory already built, proposed, or only conceptually described?

## Automatic caution flags

- elevated entry conditions
- multi-side deck systems
- large canopy or carport connections
- podium or raised-platform layouts
- retaining elements or grade changes near supports

## Result

If the accessory scope changes the support path or requires custom detailing, move the case away from a simple letter-only assumption.
