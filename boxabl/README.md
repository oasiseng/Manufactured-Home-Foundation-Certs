# Boxabl Lane

This lane separates Boxabl-specific work from standard manufactured-home foundation certification cases.

The goal is not to assume that every Boxabl job follows the same rules as a conventional manufactured-home certification letter. Instead, this lane captures the product-specific intake requirements, review questions, and deliverable boundaries that appear in Boxabl-oriented work.

## Use this lane when

- the request is tied to a Boxabl module or product package
- the manufacturer foundation package is part of the review basis
- stairs, decks, landings, or platform conditions need product-specific coordination
- the scope is closer to stamped plans or foundation guidance than a standard lender-driven certification letter
